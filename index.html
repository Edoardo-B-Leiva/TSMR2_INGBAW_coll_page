Who are we?

Florida is an educational centre that, in the past years has been adscrited to the University of Valencia. Florida also offers a variety of study paths and studies all the way from Vocational Educations to University Degrees, such as tourism, and 3D animation which vary between grant maintained and private.



Apart from this, Florida is involved in a part of "Florida Centre de Formació" and "Sociedad Cooperativa Valenciana", a project with more than 40 years of experience in the education sector that has been growing in recent years and is currently part of the Florida Group.
