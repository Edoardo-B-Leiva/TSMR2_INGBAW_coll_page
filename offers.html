TSMR Vocational Education

TSMR (Microcomputer Systems and Networks)

TSMR is a two-year Vocational Education course which teaches the fundamentals of IT, programming basics, networking and operating systems, giving lessons on Microsoft Windows and Linux, protocols and basic BASH Scripting.

Second Degree

After completing the TSMR Vocational Education, you will be rewarded with the Technician in Microcomputer Systems and Networking Certificate.
You will have access to three major two-year courses, which are:

ASIR

In this course, you will learn how to manage Computer Networks and Servers alike. Apart from this, Cybersecurity is also a major point of this course.

DAW

DAW focuses towards Web development using technologies such as PHP, Javascript and React.

DAM

DAM focuses towards Multiplatform Application Development. During this course, it's taught how todevelop mobile and desktop applications and the final project consists on creating a Mobile Application.

Double certificate!

Do you feel brave enough to get two certificates in just three years??
Then the DAW + DAM double titulaton is just for you. Learn all the skills from both degrees.
It may be challenging, but the reward is completely worth the effort.
