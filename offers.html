Medium Degree

TSMR (Mycrocomputer Systems and Networks)

TSMR is a two-year course held in Florida which teaches the students about the fundamentals of IT, some programming, knowledge about networks and its construction and operating systems, incuding lessons on how to work with Windows and Linux, their protocols and some basic bash scripting taught in the second year.

Second Degree

After completing the medium degree of TSMR, you will be rewarded with the technician in microcomputer systems and networks titulation. You will have access to three different second degrees, which are:

ASIR

In this second degree, you will learn about the adinistration of different networks and servers. Apart from this, security is also a very important part of the learning plan in this degree.

DAW

DAW shares the first course with DAM as the same subjects are coursed during it. But during the second course DAW specializes towards web design, incuding data bases.

DAM

DAM specializes towards the part of applications. During this degree, you will learn how to design mobile and desktop applications and the final project will be to create a mobile App.

Double titulation!

Do you feel brave enough to get two titulations in only three years ?? Then the DAW and DAM double titulaton is for you. Learn all the skills from each degree in less time. It may be challenging, but the rewarding will be great.
