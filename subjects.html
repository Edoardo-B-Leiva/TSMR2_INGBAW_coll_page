Subjects

Students at Florida are taught a variety of subjects that are divided between the different kinds of formation. For example, in TSMR Vocational Education, students are taught subjects involving networking and IT, with some extra subjects related to the English language and preparation to the job market.

Do you want to discover any subject on their own? What are you waiting for? Scroll below and get access to an area with plenty of information about the subjects where you will be able to find out what you will learn in each one of them and discover if ITC is the right choice for you.
